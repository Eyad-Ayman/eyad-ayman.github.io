Hey there!
I'm Eyad Ayman Hemdan, a 20-year-old from Giza, Egypt, deeply immersed in graphic design, video editing, and 3D art. My journey into the creative world began in 2017 when I first started learning Photoshop, and by 2019, I had officially started exploring graphic design and video editing. Armed with Adobe's powerful tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere Pro, I craft visuals and videos that captivate and inspire. Whether it's a striking graphic, a dynamic video edit, or pushing the boundaries of 3D art using Blender, my goal is always to create impactful experiences. While studying Marketing at Cairo University's Faculty of Commerce, I'm blending creative thinking with strategic insights to elevate my work further. Born on June 13, 2004, I thrive on pushing creative boundaries and turning ideas into reality. If you're ready to bring your vision to life, let's collaborate and create something extraordinary together.
